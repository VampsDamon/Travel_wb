Hawa Mahal

Also known as the Palace of Winds, Hawa Mahal is a mesmerizing palace in Jaipur, Rajasthan, featuring unique latticed windows designed to allow royal ladies to observe street festivities while maintaining their privacy.

MORE

Jal Mahal

Jal Mahal, or Water Palace, is an architectural gem in Jaipur, situated in Man Sagar Lake. Built in the 18th century, it beautifully combines Rajput and Mughal styles, creating a picturesque sight surrounded by the Aravalli hills. Although boat rides aren't offered.

MORE

JANTAR MANTAR

Located in Jaipur, Jantar Mantar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, hosting a collection of architectural astronomical instruments built by Maharaja Jai Singh II in the 18th century, showcasing the advancement of Indian astronomy.

MORE

NAHARGARH

Nestled on the Aravalli hills, Nahargarh Fort is a historic fortress overlooking Jaipur. Built in the 18th century, it offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Pink City and its surroundings.

MORE

Amer Fort

Situated atop a hill, Amer Fort is a majestic fort complex in Jaipur. Known for its impressive architecture, intricate marble work, and artistic blend of Hindu and Rajput styles, it is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

MORE

JAWAHAR CIRCLE

A popular public space in Jaipur, Jawahar Circle features a striking rose garden and a prominent cenotaph. It provides a tranquil setting for relaxation, leisure activities, and social gatherings.
